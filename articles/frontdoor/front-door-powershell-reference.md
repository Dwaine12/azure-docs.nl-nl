### [Nieuwe AzureRmFrontDoorFireWallPolicy](New-AzureRmFrontDoorFireWallPolicy.md)
WAF-beleid maken

### [Nieuwe AzureRmFrontDoorFrontendEndpointObject](New-AzureRmFrontDoorFrontendEndpointObject.md)

### [Nieuwe AzureRmFrontDoorRuleGroupOverrideObject](New-AzureRmFrontDoorRuleGroupOverrideObject.md)
